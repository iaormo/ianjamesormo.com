What you can expect.

I write once a week. Sometimes twice if something won’t let me sleep. No push notifications, no urgency, no one–weird–trick. Just a letter from one person who’s been rebuilding — to another who probably is too.

Each letter sits somewhere between faith, freelancing, fatherhood, and the slow work of becoming someone you’d actually want to be. If that sounds like you, you’re in the right place. If it doesn’t, there’s an unsubscribe link at the bottom and no hard feelings.

While you wait for the first letter — here’s somewhere to start. The daily devotional, or the musings.
