The invitation

If you have opened five of these letters, you are the reader I was writing for before I knew you existed.

I have a book. It is called You Are Not Finished, and it is the long form of what these letters are the short form of. Grace, grit, and the long way home. A parking lot in the city of smiles. A wife who remained. A man who is still being rebuilt and is not pretending to be finished.

Read You Are Not Finished

If the letters have earned any trust, that is the book. No urgency theater, no countdown timer, no bonus package stapled to the side. One link. One reader. One decision.

From next week, these letters drop to one a week. A single Musing, a single question, the door left open if you want to write back. That is the rhythm from here.

Thanks for being here. I do not take it for granted.

— Ian
